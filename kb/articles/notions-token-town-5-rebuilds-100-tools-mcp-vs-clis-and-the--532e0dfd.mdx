## Summary

Swyx interviews Sarah Sachs and Simon Last from Notion about their journey in developing Custom Agents, exploring the challenges and innovations in AI tooling for productivity.

## Key Insights

> "Why early agent attempts failed: no tool-calling standard, short context windows, unreliable models, and too much complexity exposed to the model."
>
> — Discussing the challenges faced during the development of Notion's Custom Agents.

> "How Notion thinks about roadmap timing: not swimming upstream against model limitations, but also building early enough that the product is ready when the models are."
>
> — Explaining Notion's strategic approach to product development in relation to AI models.

> "Notion cares about teaching the top of the class, building for sophisticated operators rather than abstracting away too much capability for everyone."
>
> — Highlighting Notion's focus on advanced users in their product design.
