## Full Article

While you could debate ARR revenue recognition, it is hard to deny very real reports of secondary market and traditional media reporting that Anthropic, after their miracle Q1 of 80x annualized growth and one month jump of $15B ARR, is now being valued at $1-1.2T, making it officially overtake OpenAI as the 11th-15th most valuable company in the world.This is a REVENUE, not a financial speculation, chart: All this and while Block (40%), Coinbase (14%), and Cloudflare (20%) have laid off massive swathes of their workforce, all citing AI readiness. Its hard to tell the degree to which this is AI-washing normal layoffs, but it is clear that stronger companies, like Linear, are the ones that grow, not shrink, due to AI. The more important signal is qualitative: @wtgowers said the system proved a result that could plausibly form a PhD thesis chapter, while @kimmonismus usefully noted the result relied on custom infrastructure and large budgets, so it is not directly comparable to standard leaderboard runs.
