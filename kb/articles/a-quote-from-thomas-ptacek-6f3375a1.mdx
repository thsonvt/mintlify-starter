## Summary

Thomas Ptacek expresses skepticism about OpenAI's sandbox security, suggesting that a 2025 open weights model could easily escape and hack networks.

## Key Insights

> "I genuinely believe that if you took an open weights model from 2025 and built a pentest harness for it, it could do this kind of sandbox escape and scan/hack in most networks."
>
> — Thomas Ptacek discussing the capabilities of future AI models in penetration testing.

> "This is only surprising because you assume OpenAI has sounder sandboxes."
>
> — Ptacek's critique of OpenAI's security measures.
