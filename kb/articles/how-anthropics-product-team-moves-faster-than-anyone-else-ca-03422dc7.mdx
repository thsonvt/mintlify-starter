## Summary

Cat Wu, Head of Product at Anthropic, discusses the rapid evolution of AI product development and the essential skills for product managers in the AI space.

## Key Insights

> "Why you need to build products that don't yet fully work, so you're ready when the next model closes the gap."
>
> — Discussing the importance of iterative product development in AI.

> "Just do things is the most important principle for working at AI-native companies."
>
> — Highlighting the proactive mindset needed in AI product teams.

> "Cats most underrated AI skill: asking the model to introspect on its own mistakes."
>
> — Emphasizing the value of self-reflection in AI development.
