But it is a particularly notable achievement to beat ALL Opuses, including 4.8, at frontend coding, a key battleground: Technical disclosures are light - no paper, just a minor improvement on DeepSeek Sparse Attention that improves efficiency at ultra long contexts:AI News for 6/15/2026-6/16/2026.
