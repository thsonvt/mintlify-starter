## Summary

Ken Jin announces that the CPython JIT has met its performance goals ahead of schedule for macOS AArch64 and x86_64 Linux.

## Key Insights

> "The 3.15 alpha JIT is about 11-12% faster on macOS AArch64 than the tail calling interpreter."
>
> — Ken Jin discussing the performance improvements of the CPython JIT.

> "5-6% faster than the standard interpreter on x86_64 Linux."
>
> — Ken Jin highlighting the performance gains for Linux users.
