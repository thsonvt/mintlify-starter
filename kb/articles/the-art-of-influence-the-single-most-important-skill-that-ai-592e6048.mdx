## Summary

Jessica Fain discusses the importance of influence in decision-making and how AI will enhance, rather than replace, this skill in leadership.

## Key Insights

> "Why you should go in to learn, not to convince."
>
> — On the mindset needed for effective influence.

> "AI will make influence more important, not less."
>
> — Discussing the evolving role of influence in the age of AI.

> "That's so interesting. What led you to believe that?"
>
> — The best question to ask when a leader says something that seems wrong.
