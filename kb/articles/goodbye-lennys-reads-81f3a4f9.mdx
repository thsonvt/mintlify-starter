## Full Article

After a year of bringing you these audio versions of my newsletter, Ive decided its time to wind down this experiment. Its been a lot of fun making these episodes and its been incredible to see how far the technology has developed!Im going to keep all of the Lennys Reads episodes up on this channel for another week. Thank you so much for listening, it's been great having you along for the ride.If you want to keep up with all my content, make sure to keep up to date here: Twitter: https://twitter.com/lennysanLinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/lennyrachitsky/Podcast: https://www.youtube.com/@LennysPodcast Newsletter: http://lennysnewsletter.com/I'll see you there.
