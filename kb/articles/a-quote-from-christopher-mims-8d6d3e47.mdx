## Summary

Christopher Mims critiques the idea of giving AI total control over personal lives, suggesting it will seem foolish in hindsight.

> ""give AI total control of my computer and therefore my entire life""
>

>
> — Mims compares the decision to a cultural reference to highlight its absurdity.
