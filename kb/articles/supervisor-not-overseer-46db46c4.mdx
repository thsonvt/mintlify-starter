```
# Supervisor, not overseer
```

**Author**: Simon Willison  
**Published**: 2026-02-12  
**Source**: [https://simonwillison.net/2026/Feb/12/supervisor/#atom-everything](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Feb/12/supervisor/#atom-everything)

---

In my [post about my Showboat project](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Feb/10/showboat-and-rodney/) I used the term "overseer" to refer to the person who manages a coding agent. It turns out that's a term tied to [slavery and plantation management](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Plantations_in_the_American_South#Overseer). So that's gross! I've edited that post to use "supervisor" instead, and I'll be using that going forward.
