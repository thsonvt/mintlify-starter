## Summary

Simon Willison shares a quote from Julia Evans about writing for a specific audience, often envisioning a past version of oneself.

## Key Insights

> "Instead, I picture a specific person and I just write for them."
>
> — Julia Evans discusses her approach to writing.

> "Often this person is 'me, but 3 years ago' or a good friend."
>
> — Julia Evans elaborates on who she envisions while writing.
