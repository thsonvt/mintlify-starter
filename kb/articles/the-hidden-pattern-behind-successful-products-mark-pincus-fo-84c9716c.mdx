## Summary

Mark Pincus, founder of Zynga, shares insights on creating successful consumer products in his upcoming book, emphasizing a framework for innovation and the importance of instincts.

## Key Insights

> "Kill hope before hope kills you"
>
> — Discussing the importance of realistic expectations in product development.

> "Your instincts are right 95% of the time, but your ideas are wrong 75% of the time"
>
> — Highlighting the value of trusting one's instincts in the creative process.

> "Copy what's proven, make it better so that 10 out of 10 people say f*ck yes, I'll use this then add something new"
>
> — Describing his Proven, Better, New framework for product success.
