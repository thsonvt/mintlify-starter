## Full Article

19th February 2026 LadybirdBrowser/ladybird: Abandon Swift adoption (via) Back in August 2024 the Ladybird browser project announced an intention to adopt Swift as their memory-safe language of choice. As of this commit it looks like they've changed their mind: Everywhere: Abandon Swift adoption After making no progress on this for a very long time, let's acknowledge it's not going anywhere and remove it from the codebase.
