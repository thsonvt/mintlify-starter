## Summary

The annual Tech Worker Sentiment Survey reveals a divided tech workforce regarding AI, with significant concerns about burnout and job satisfaction.

## Key Insights

> "AI has split the tech workforce almost exactly in half."
>
> — Discussing the division in feelings towards AI among tech workers.

> "Burnout has jumped an alarming 11 points in a single year."
>
> — Highlighting the increase in burnout rates among tech workers.

> "Nobody in tech would recommend their job to someone entering the industry today."
>
> — Reflecting the overall dissatisfaction in the tech job market.
