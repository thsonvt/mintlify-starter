## Full Article

Brian Grinstead is a distinguished engineer at Mozilla, where hes worked on Firefox and the web platform since 2013 (he joined to help launch Firefox DevTools). Recently he and his team pointed an agentic bug-finding pipeline at Firefoxa codebase with tens of thousands of files and tens of millions of lines of codeand shipped a record month of security fixes. The viral chart everyone saw gave the credit to Anthropics new Mythos model.
